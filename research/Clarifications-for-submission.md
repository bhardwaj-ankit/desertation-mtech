This is an important communication regarding the submission of your Abstract/Outline Report on the Viva Portal for your Dissertation/Project/Project Work Semester.

1. Clarifications on Project Abstract (or) Project Outline Report:
Please note that the Project Abstract and Project Outline Report are the same documents.
The Project Abstract (or) Project Outline Report needs to be uploaded by you on the Viva portal before the deadline. The soft Submission date for abstract report is 5thMay 2026(Tuesday) and the hard Submission date for abstract report is 10th May 2026 (Sunday).
This is also a gentle reminder for the submission of your Project Abstract or Project Outline Report before the Hard Submission date of 10th May 2026 (Sunday).
Kindly refer to the steps mentioned under the section “Steps for Submitting Abstract/Outline Report for Dissertation/Project/Project Work Semester” for uploading your Project Abstract or Project Outline Report on the Viva Portal.

2. Clarifications on Soft Submission and Hard Submission Date:
Soft Submission Date: This is the date by which you are expected to start submitting your Project Outline Report, Mid Semester Report, or Final Report. Submissions are accepted from this date.
Hard Submission Date: After this date, no further submissions will be accepted. Even submissions made after this deadline might not be considered for evaluation.
Important Note: Please ensure that all submissions are made on time. Late submissions will not be accepted, so please adhere to the specified deadlines meticulously.

Submission Instructions for Abstract/Outline Report:
Log in to the eLearn portal using your BITS eLearn Mail ID and password: eLearn Portal
Visit the “Viva/Project Work” section on the top left of the homepage.
Click on "Document Submission" and select "Abstract" from the dropdown menu.
Click on “Choose File” against the “Upload New Abstract” entry to upload your Project Abstract/Outline Report in PDF format (≤10 MB).
Soft Submission Date: 5th May 2026
Hard Submission Date: 10th May 2026
